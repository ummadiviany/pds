Learning about how computers work is essential to effective use of computers. 
Computers run programs that are written in a programming language. 
Learning atleast one programming language is important in todays world.
This course will cover the basics of programming languages and how to use them to build real world applications.

During this 12-week course, you will learn to automate your daily tasks with a computer, solve numerical problems, working with tons of data (tabular & medical images) and make your life easier.
Through weekly hands-on assignments and final project, you will acquire the toolset to solve real world computational problems.
